Learn to draw anime!

A small effort to provide a free resource to learn to draw anime, to fight the lack of english courses on the topic.

This course is still a work in progress, as such most topics aren't yet covered. As time goes on I'll try to cover the basics of drawing as well as drawing anime character, props and environments.
